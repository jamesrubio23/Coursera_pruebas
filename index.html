About Us

This restaurant is a place where you can enjoy the best of Italian cuisine, and also have some great food. The menu is very simple, but it has many different dishes that are served in different ways. The food served here is pure halal.

This restaurant is located on the main street of Milan, which means that there are no restaurants in this area that serve halal food. The restaurant is open at all times, every day, so that you can enjoy your meal with friends or family anytime, any day!
